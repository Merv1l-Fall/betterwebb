Where is the event taking place?

Find us here!

The Better Web conference will be held at the Gothenburg Convention Centre, a premier venue located in the vibrant heart of Gothenburg. This modern facility is designed to foster creativity and collaboration, featuring spacious seminar rooms equipped with cutting-edge audio-visual technology to enhance the attendee experience.

Conveniently situated near the picturesque Gothenburg Harbor, the convention centre is easily accessible via public transportation, with tram and bus stations just a short walk away. Participants will find a variety of nearby restaurants, cafés, and hotels, making it easy to enjoy the local culture and cuisine during the event.

Who are we?

We are NextGen Web Solutions

NextGen Web Solutions is a pioneering technology company at the forefront of web development, dedicated to shaping the future of digital experiences. Founded by a team of seasoned developers, designers, and digital strategists, NextGen focuses on delivering innovative web solutions that prioritize performance, accessibility, and user engagement.

At NextGen Web Solutions, we believe that the future of web development is bright and full of possibilities. However, with rapid technological advancements and evolving user expectations, it's essential for professionals in the field to stay informed and connected. This is why we decided to host the Better Web conference.
